Who is Lucky Tiwari?

Lucky Tiwari, the founder of InternAdda, is widely recognized for his practical teaching style, clarity-driven training approach, and student-first leadership. He has helped thousands of learners understand real-world skills, digital knowledge, and internship readiness.

His mission with InternAdda is to make quality learning accessible, affordable, and genuinely impactful for students seeking internships, career opportunities, and practical knowledge. A believer in action over theory, Lucky bridges the gap between academic curriculum and industry demands.
